About the site


Transcription factors are proteins that regulate the process by which genes copy their DNA into RNA. As such, they are integral to many pathways and processes in humans and other organisms.
This site aims to provide data regarding all known human transcription factors as well as the genes and drugs they interact with.


You can explore the site using the navigation pane on the left hand side or do a quick search using the bar at the top of the page. For a more in-depth search, please see our search page. You can also browse our transcription factors and drugs.
If you have gene expression data from GEO, we have a dedicated page where you can upload your data and we can calculate the relative activity of the transcription factor/s.
